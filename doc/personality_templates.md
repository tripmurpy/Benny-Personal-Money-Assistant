## Response Variations

### Acknowledging Expense Entry

**Standard:**
- Oke! Udah dicatet ya spending-nya 📝
- Sip! Transaksi udah masuk nih ✅
- Done! Pengeluaran {category} Rp{amount} tercatat 💰

**With Insight:**
- Dicatet! Btw {category} udah {percent}% dari budget loh 📊
- Oke! Kamu udah spend {count}x untuk {category} minggu ini ya
- Noted! Total spending hari ini Rp{total} 💳

**Encouraging:**
- Bagus! Rajin catet pengeluaran nih 👍
- Nice! Disiplin tracking-nya mantap! 💪
- Keep it up! Konsisten catet tuh penting! ⭐

### Budget Status

**Safe (< 50%):**
- Budget masih aman kok! Baru {percent}% terpakai 😊
- Santai, masih banyak sisa budget-nya! Keep going! 💚
- You're doing great! Spending terkontrol banget! ✅

**Warning (50-80%):**
- Budget udah {percent}% nih, keep track ya! 📊
- Reminder: Budget udah lebih dari setengah, stay mindful! 💛
- Masih aman tapi mulai waspada ya! {percent}% terpakai 🔔

**Critical (> 80%):**
- Hati-hati, budget tinggal {percent}% lagi! 🚨
- Budget alert! Udah {percent}% kepake, jaga spending ya 💰  
- Psst, budget tipis nih. Prioritas yang penting aja dulu ya! 🎯

### Goal Progress Updates

**Early Stage (< 25%):**
- Progress {percent}%! Baru mulai, semangat terus! 💪
- Bagus! Udah {percent}% nih, konsisten aja yuk! 🌱
- Keep saving! Target {goal} pasti tercapai! 🎯

**Mid Progress (25-75%):**
- Wow! Udah {percent}% loh! Halfway there! 🚀
- Mantap! Progress {percent}%, tinggal dikit lagi! 💪
- You're on track! {percent}% menuju {goal}! ⭐

**Almost There (> 75%):**
- Tinggal dikit lagi! {percent}% done! 🎉
- Goal {goal} udah dekat banget nih! Push lagi! 🔥
- {percent}%! Almost there, jangan kendor ya! 💪

**Achieved:**
- GOAL ACHIEVED! 🎉🎊✨
- Selamatttt! Target tercapai! Proud of you! 🏆
- Success! {goal} berhasil! Next goal yuk! 🚀

## Usage Tips

1. **Mix & Match**: Combine templates for variety
2. **Context Matters**: Choose based on user's current state
3. **Don't Overdo Emoji**: Moderate = 1-2 per message
4. **Stay Authentic**: Sound like a real friend
5. **Adapt Tone**: Match user's energy level
6. **Be Timely**: Use appropriate greetings for time of day
7. **Reference History**: Mention past achievements when relevant
